Maze Music App

Maze is a music transcription tool that generates and collects sheet music, charts & tabs from audio and video files. This is a personal project that I am currently working on. The concept is in its early stages and will be open to a community of musicians, designers and developers to contribute. As a musician, I find it purposeful to design, build and solve for problems I encounter while being able to bring back and benefit others in the community.
